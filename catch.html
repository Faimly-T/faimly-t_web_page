What Catch isQué es Catch

A student with the right mentor doesn't just find scholarships — they win them. Catch is that mentor, powered by AI and available to everyone: it finds the right opportunities, then guides every step from first essay to acceptance letter. Un estudiante con el mentor adecuado no solo encuentra becas — las gana.
